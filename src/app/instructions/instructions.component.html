Welcome to the Instruction section.

Please read the following instructions before procedding to test

  1. You will be given 5 seconds to choose your favourite quiz from the given drop down list at the top right corner else HTML Quiz will be loaded by default.
  2. Upon selecting the quiz you will be redirected to quiz section.
  3. Only one answer is correct
  4. You can review the test anytime between the test.
    • One shown in Yellow are pending once
    • One shown in green are attempted one
  5. Attempting all the question is not required
  6. Upon submission you will be redirected to page consisiting of your marks and percentage
  7. You will get an option to see detailed result
